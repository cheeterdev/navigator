# Introducing Cheeter Navigator



## ❓ What is Cheeter Navigator?

Cheeter Navigator is **our new self-made browser** aiming to provide you with a **secure browsing** experience.



## ✨ What makes us special?

Cheeter Navigator comes with a range of **security features** that many other browsers don’t include.



## 🥷 Always Incognito

With Cheeter Navigator, you **never browse exposed**.
Navigator always uses Incognito mode and lets you toggle it **per website** with just one button.



## 💨 Disposable Mode

Disposable Mode **automatically deletes** all your browsing data **when closing the browser**, leaving no traces behind.



## 💉 Tracker Manipulation

Clicking on the Syringe icon opens tabs that **automatically browse the internet**, helping to keep your **real interests hidden** among thousands of pieces of **fake data**.
